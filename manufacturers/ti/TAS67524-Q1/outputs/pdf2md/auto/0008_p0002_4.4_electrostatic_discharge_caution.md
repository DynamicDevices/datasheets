# 4.4 Electrostatic Discharge Caution

![ESD Caution Symbol](./images/page_2_img_inline_1.png)

**Electrostatic Discharge (ESD) Warning Symbol and Precautions**

This image displays the standard ESD caution symbol, which consists of:
* A triangular warning sign with a yellow background and black border
* A black hand icon reaching toward a circuit/component symbol
* A lightning bolt or electrical discharge symbol crossing through the hand, indicating the danger of electrostatic discharge
* This symbol warns users that the device or component is sensitive to electrostatic discharge

The presence of this symbol indicates that proper ESD handling procedures must be followed when working with this equipment to prevent damage to sensitive electronic components.
