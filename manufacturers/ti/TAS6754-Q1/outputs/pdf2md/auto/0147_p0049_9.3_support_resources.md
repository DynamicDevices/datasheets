# 9.3 Support Resources

## Technical Support

For technical assistance, product information, and documentation updates, please utilize the following resources:

### Online Resources

- **Product Documentation Portal**: Access the latest datasheets, application notes, and reference designs at our online documentation center
- **Knowledge Base**: Search our comprehensive database of frequently asked questions, troubleshooting guides, and design tips
- **Community Forums**: Connect with other engineers and designers to share experiences and solutions

### Direct Support Channels

- **Technical Support Email**: Contact our applications engineering team for detailed technical inquiries
- **Phone Support**: Speak directly with a technical support engineer during business hours
- **Field Application Engineers (FAE)**: Request consultation with a local FAE for design review and optimization assistance

### Development Tools

- **Evaluation Boards**: Hardware platforms available for rapid prototyping and testing
- **Software Development Kits (SDK)**: Complete development packages including drivers, libraries, and example code
- **Design Tools**: Access to simulation models, layout guidelines, and configuration utilities

### Training and Education

- **Webinars**: Regular online training sessions covering product features and application design
- **Video Tutorials**: Step-by-step guides for common implementation scenarios
- **Technical Workshops**: In-person training events at regional locations

### Documentation Updates

All documentation is subject to updates and revisions. Please check the product page regularly for:

- Errata sheets
- Application notes
- Design guidelines
- Software updates
- Reference designs

### Feedback

We value your input to improve our products and documentation. Please submit feedback, suggestions, or report documentation issues through our online feedback form.
