# 9.5 Electrostatic Discharge Caution

Electrostatic discharge (ESD) can damage electronic components. To prevent ESD damage:

- Handle the device by its edges
- Wear a grounded wrist strap when handling the device
- Store the device in anti-static packaging when not in use
- Use properly grounded tools and workstations
- Avoid touching pins, leads, or circuitry
- Follow your facility's ESD protection procedures

Failure to follow proper ESD handling procedures may result in device damage or degraded performance.
