# 9.2 Receiving Notification of Documentation Updates

To receive automatic notification of updates to this documentation, you can subscribe to the product documentation mailing list or RSS feed provided by the manufacturer. This ensures you are always working with the most current version of the technical specifications and user guides.

## Subscription Options

- **Email Notifications**: Register your email address on the product support portal to receive direct notifications when documentation is updated.
- **RSS Feed**: Subscribe to the RSS feed available on the documentation download page for real-time updates.
- **Product Portal**: Check the product portal regularly for version history and change logs that detail all documentation revisions.

## Update Frequency

Documentation updates are typically released:

- When new product features are added
- When errata or corrections are identified
- Following major firmware or software releases
- In response to customer feedback and technical support issues

## Version Control

Always verify the document version number and revision date on the cover page or footer to ensure you are referencing the latest information. Outdated documentation may contain incorrect register values, deprecated procedures, or superseded technical specifications.
