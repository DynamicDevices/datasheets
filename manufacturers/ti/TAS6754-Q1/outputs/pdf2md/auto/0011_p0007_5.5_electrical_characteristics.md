- **Switching parameters** - Switching frequency ranges and tolerances
- **Timing specifications** - Start-up times, shutdown timing, mute/unmute response
- **Digital interface characteristics** - I2C/SPI timing requirements, logic levels
- **Protection thresholds** - Overcurrent, overtemperature, and other fault detection limits
